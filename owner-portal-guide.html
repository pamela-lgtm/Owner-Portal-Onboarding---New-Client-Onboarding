First Things First

Activate Your Owner Portal

Once Verandah has set up your account, you'll receive an email from AppFolio inviting you to activate your portal. This is a one-time process and takes just a few minutes.

01
Watch for the activation email

You'll get one of two emails to start:

  • A welcome announcement from Verandah inviting you in, or
  • A notification that an item (like your first statement) is ready to view in your portal

Either email will include an Activate Now button — click that button to begin.

If you don't see it Check your spam or junk folder. AppFolio activation emails sometimes land there. The sender will be from AppFolio's domain — not from Verandah directly.
02
Confirm your email address

The activation page will show your email address — the one Verandah has on file. If it's correct, continue. If not, contact Linda before going further so we can update it.

You'll then receive a confirmation email. Open it and click Confirm my email.

The Verandah-branded password setup screen you'll see
03
Move on to password setup

Once your email is confirmed, AppFolio will walk you straight into the next step — creating your password and setting up two-factor authentication. Don't close the window.

Security Setup

Create Your Password & Two-Factor Authentication

Your portal contains real financial data — disbursement history, banking information, statements — so AppFolio adds two layers of security. The setup is quick.

01
Choose a strong password

On the password page, create a password that's unique to this portal — not one you reuse from email or banking. Check the box agreeing to AppFolio's Terms of Service, then click Create an account.

Password manager users Use one. 1Password, Bitwarden, Apple Keychain, Google Password Manager — any of them. They generate strong passwords and store them so you'll never have to remember this one.
02
Set up two-factor authentication

AppFolio will ask for a phone number to send your security code to. Enter your primary mobile number — the phone you have with you most often.

Choose how you'd like to receive verification codes:

  • SMS text message (most common)
  • Phone call (works for landlines too)
Important — Email 2FA No Longer Supported AppFolio recently retired email-based two-factor authentication for security reasons. If you're a returning AppFolio user with another property manager, you may be prompted to migrate to phone-based verification. Just follow the prompts — same idea, more secure.
03
Confirm with your verification code

AppFolio will text or call you with a verification code. Enter it to confirm your phone is registered.

You'll see an option to "remember this device for 30 days." Checking this means you won't need to enter a code every single time you log in from this same phone or computer — only periodically.

Tip from Linda Check that "remember this device" box on your trusted phone and home computer. It saves you time without compromising security.
04
You're in

Click Log In, and you'll land on your dashboard for the first time. We'll walk through what you're looking at next.

A Tour of Your Portal

What You'll See When You Log In

Your portal is organized around two main areas: a dashboard that summarizes your property's performance at a glance, and a set of tabs for diving deeper into specifics.

The Dashboard

Your dashboard is the home base — a real-time view of how your property is performing. It's broken into widgets, each one summarizing a different aspect of your investment:

Total Income

All rental income and other receipts collected against your property.

Total Expense

All paid bills against your property — maintenance, HOA, insurance pass-throughs, etc.

Your Contribution

The lump sum of all contributions you've made toward your property (reserves, capital expenses).

Your Disbursement

The lump sum of all distributions Verandah has paid out to you.

Most Recent Packet

Your latest owner statement (mid-month or end-of-month). Click "See All" to view your full history.

Occupancy

Whether your property is occupied or vacant as of today.

Rent Collection

The total rent charged versus rent collected so far this month.

Income & Expense Breakdowns

A line-by-line view of each transaction making up your totals — full transparency.

Customize what you see The dashboard is yours. Click Customize in the top right to add, remove, resize, or rearrange widgets. Save the layout you prefer, and it'll stick across every device you log in from. If you ever want a clean slate, click Restore to Default.

The Tabs

Along the side of your portal you'll see a set of tabs. Here's what each one is for:

Dashboard
Your home base — the customizable overview described above.
Reports
Run real-time reports for your property. Filter by date range, search within results, export to PDF/Excel/CSV.
Properties
A summary of your active properties: address, occupancy, lease end date, rental amount, and any reserve balance held.
Statements
Where you'll spend most of your time. View or download every owner packet Verandah has published — both your mid-month and end-of-month statements. Most recent at top, organized by owner of record.
Documents
Any other files Verandah has shared with you — leases, inspection reports, insurance documents, 1099s, anything we've published.
Contributions
Make a secure payment to your reserve via credit/debit card or eCheck. Only appears if enabled by Verandah.
A note on what you'll see first Brand new owners often log in and find very little data displayed — that's normal. Until your property has been listed, leased, and the first rent has been collected, there's nothing to populate. Within the first few months, your portal will start filling in.
Statements & Documents

Reading Your Owner Statements

At Verandah Properties, we issue two owner statements per month for every property under management. This is part of our proactive communication standard — we want you to have full visibility into your property's financial activity in real time, not only at month-end.

A
Mid-Month Statement — Issued at the time of your owner distribution

Typically around the 8th–10th of the month. This statement is published when your monthly owner distribution (ACH disbursement) is sent. It covers all activity from the 1st of the month through the disbursement date and shows you:

  • Beginning cash balance and your required reserve (the reserve amount is established in your Property Management Agreement)
  • Rent received from the tenant for the current month
  • Any program fees deducted (e.g., SureVestor General Liability, Animal Protection Guarantee, if applicable)
  • Verandah's management fee for the month
  • Your owner distribution (ACH payment) and ending balance

The purpose of the mid-month statement is to give you a clear, itemized view of exactly how your distribution was calculated — the moment funds reach your account.

B
End-of-Month Statement — Issued at the close of the month

This statement reflects the full month's transactions through the final calendar day. It will mirror the mid-month statement if no additional activity occurred, or it will capture any items posted after disbursement — for example, a maintenance invoice paid from reserves or a vendor payment processed mid-month.

The purpose of the end-of-month statement is to provide a complete, reconciled record of the property's full monthly financial activity for your records.

→
A note on disbursement timing

Per your Property Management Agreement, owner distributions are processed once per month, as soon as tenant funds have cleared our trust account, in accordance with Florida law.

We typically target the 8th–10th of the month as the standard disbursement window, though the exact date may shift based on:

  • Weekends and holidays
  • ACH processing times
  • When tenant payment clears our account
Where to find them Both statements are posted directly to your Owner Portal under the Statements tab. All historical statements remain accessible in the portal for your records — you'll never lose visibility into past months.
01
Find the Statements tab

Click Statements in the side menu. You'll see every packet Verandah has published, with the most recent at the top. Mid-month and end-of-month statements appear in chronological order alongside one another.

02
Open or download

Click Download Packet on any statement to save it to your device. Each packet is a complete PDF — easy to read, easy to forward to your accountant, easy to file.

03
For tax season — find your 1099

At year-end, your 1099 will be published to the Documents tab. If you've consented to electronic delivery (most owners do), this is the fastest way to get it to your accountant — just download and forward.

Tip from Linda Many owners create a "Verandah" folder on their computer or in cloud storage and drop each downloaded packet there as it arrives. By tax time, everything you'll need is already organized.
04
Questions about anything you see?

Reach out to our Client Experience team. Owner statements can take a couple of cycles to feel intuitive — and Verandah would rather walk you through one line by line than have you wonder. No question is too small.

Funding & Reserves

Making a Contribution

If you ever need to add funds to your property's reserve account — to cover an upcoming repair, a capital expense, or just to maintain a healthy operating cushion — you can do it directly through your portal.

01
Click the Contributions tab

From the side menu, click Contributions.

Note If you don't see this tab, contact our Client Experience team — it may need to be enabled in your account settings.
02
Enter the contribution details

Select your property, enter an amount (up to $15,000 per transaction), and choose your payment method:

  • eCheck (ACH) — lowest fees, takes 3–5 business days to clear
  • Credit or debit card — instant, but transaction fees apply

AppFolio will show you the exact transaction fee before you submit. There are no hidden costs.

03
Review and submit

Confirm the details on the review screen, then click Submit. You'll get an email confirmation right away.

For your records Save the confirmation email or print the receipt — handy at tax time. The contribution and the transaction fee appear as separate line items on your statement (the contribution from Verandah, the fee from AppFolio).

Updating Your Bank Information

If your banking details change — new bank, new account number, anything — please reach out to our Client Experience team and we'll handle it for you. Here's how it works:

  1. Email clientexperience@verandahproperties.com letting us know you'd like to update your account information.
  2. Our team will send you an ACH Authorization form through AppFolio Forms.
  3. Complete and return the form. Verandah's accounting team will then update the account information on file for your future disbursements.
Why this process Bank account changes go through a secure form rather than direct portal entry — this ensures every update is properly authorized and documented in your records.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

I never received my activation email — what now?

First, check your spam or junk folder. Activation emails come from AppFolio's domain (not Verandah's), and some email providers route them aggressively.

If it's not there, email Linda. She can confirm the correct email is on file and resend the activation link in seconds.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the AppFolio login page, click Forgot Password? and enter your email. AppFolio will send you a reset link.

If the reset email doesn't arrive, contact Linda — sometimes it's a sign your email on file isn't current.

What if I'm traveling internationally and can't receive verification codes?

You have a few options:

  • Enable international roaming on your phone — SMS and calls work normally
  • Use Google Voice — works over Wi-Fi anywhere in the world. AppFolio fully supports Google Voice numbers for 2FA.
  • Add an international phone number to your account temporarily

If you'll be traveling for an extended period, let Linda know in advance and we can plan ahead.

I own properties under multiple LLCs but only see one in my portal.

Access to your portal is tied to your email address. As long as Verandah has the same email on file for each entity, all your properties should show up in one portal — scroll down on the Statements tab to see separate packets for each entity.

If you're missing properties you expect to see, contact our Client Experience team. We may need to harmonize the email addresses across your records.

Can I use the same login on my phone and computer?

Yes — your portal is fully responsive, meaning it works just as well on a phone or tablet as it does on a computer. The login URL and credentials are the same.

Two-factor authentication will ask you to verify each new device once. After that, if you've checked "remember this device for 30 days," you'll go straight in.

The numbers on my dashboard look wrong. What do I do?

Don't troubleshoot it alone. Email our Client Experience team and tell them exactly which figure looks off. We'd much rather walk you through it than have you wonder. Sometimes what looks like an error is a timing question (e.g., a payment cleared yesterday but a statement was generated the day before). Other times it's something we need to correct on our end. Either way, we want to know.

Are international phone numbers supported for 2FA?

Yes. AppFolio supports a wide range of international country codes — you'll see them in the dropdown when setting up your phone. (One known issue: codes don't always reach phone numbers in China; AppFolio has been working to resolve that.)

Is my data safe?

AppFolio is the industry-standard property management platform used by tens of thousands of professional property managers across North America. Your portal uses HTTPS encryption end-to-end, mandatory two-factor authentication, and is independently audited for security.

Verandah and AppFolio both treat your financial data with the same care a bank does — because for most owners, that's exactly what it is.

What if I lose access to the phone I used for two-factor authentication?

Contact our Client Experience team right away (or Linda directly if you're still in the onboarding phase). Verandah's admin can help reset your account so you can register a new phone — there's a verification process, but it's straightforward.

How often will I receive statements?

Verandah publishes two statements per month for every property — a mid-month statement issued at the time of your owner distribution (typically the 8th–10th), and an end-of-month statement issued at the close of the month. You'll receive an email notification each time a new statement is ready.

This is part of our proactive communication standard — we want you to have full visibility into your property's financial activity in real time, not only at month-end.
